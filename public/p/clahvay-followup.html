2

Map & merge with BigCommerce data

Student profiles, class history, active pass balances, attendance records, purchase history — everything transfers into your new PostgreSQL database. Students get a simple invite to set up their account using their existing email. One click, they're in. Their full history is preserved.

Run both systems in parallel, then cut over

You keep Pike13 and BigCommerce running until you're 100% comfortable. We run both side by side. When you're confident everything works — and only when you say so — we point clahvay.com to the new platform and cancel the old subscriptions. No pressure, no deadline.
