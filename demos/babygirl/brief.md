## Brand Voice & Vibe
- Self-described: "A calming neighborhood diner. Coffee early, food all day, lights low when evening comes."
- Google tagline: "All day dining. Cafe. Restaurant. Bar."
- Opening poster: "Let me cook! Good food! Real vibes."
- Warm, neighborhood-first, laid-back but elevated
- Community angle: hosted Side by Side Recovery Brunch for stroke & brain injury survivors

## Brand Colors & Visual Identity
- **Primary:** Bold chartreuse/yellow-green — dominates IG graphics, promotional tiles, interior window panes
- **Secondary:** Black — used in P.M. menu cards and logo
- **Accent:** Warm orange, red, amber — from stained glass window panels
- **Background soft:** Dusty rose/salmon pink — used on grand opening poster
- **Logo:** Black handwritten "babygirl" lettering inside white circle on white brick wall
- **Typography:** Mix of bold retro/comic-book illustration, clean minimalist type-only menus, slightly grungy handwritten vibe
- **Photography aesthetic:** Dark, moody, warm-toned food photography; bright airy interiors in daylight
